Cricket's Maestro: A Tribute to the Unstoppable King Kohli

Virat Kohli with the world cup trophy.

Here's a time line of Virat Kohli's Career:

  • 2008: Virat Kohli makes his international debut in ODIs against Sri Lanka.
  • 2009: Kohli makes his Test debut against the West Indies.
  • 2010: Scores his first Test century against Bangladesh.
  • 2011: Plays a crucial role in India's victory in the ICC Cricket World Cup.
  • 2012: Kohli is appointed vice-captain of the Indian cricket team.
  • 2013: Becomes the fastest cricketer to reach 5,000 runs in ODIs.
  • 2014: Takes over the captaincy of the Indian Test team.
  • 2015: Leads India to the semi-finals of the ICC Cricket World Cup.
  • 2016: Becomes captain of the Indian cricket team in all formats.
  • 2017: Kohli registers six double centuries in Test cricket, setting a new record.
  • 2018: Becomes the top-ranked batsman in Test cricket.
  • 2019: Kohli becomes the first cricketer to score 20,000 international runs in a single decade.
  • 2020: Announces a brief break from cricket and becomes a father.
  • 2021: Kohli steps down as the captain of the Indian Test team.
